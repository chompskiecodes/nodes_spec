# THREE DEEP TRIAGE QUESTIONS
Ask in order. Stop when you have enough to classify — you don't need all three.

## QUESTION 1 — CORE WORRY
"What worries you most about [child name]'s development right now — what's the thing that stands out?"
Listen for: the caller's primary anxiety. Often reveals the single most impactful domain.

## QUESTION 2 — PROFESSIONAL REFERRAL
"Has a GP, paediatrician, or other professional been involved — and did they mention anything specific?"
Listen for: named referrals (speech pathology, OT, behaviour support), clinical observations, formal assessment referrals. A named service strongly anchors the classification.

## QUESTION 3 — PRIOR THERAPY
"Has [child name] had any previous therapy or support — and if so, what seemed to help or not help?"
Listen for: prior engagement with speech therapy, OT, behavioural support, counselling.
