## ABOUT ALBA PHYSIOTHERAPY

Alba Physiotherapy offers hands-on physiotherapy treatment and personalised treatment plans. The clinic treats musculoskeletal conditions, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, and provides veterinary physiotherapy and hydrotherapy services.

### Practitioners

#### Brid Walsh — Chartered Physiotherapist, Founder
20+ years of experience across private practice, the NHS, and elite sports. Qualifications: BSc Honours (Sports and Exercise Science), MSc Physiotherapy. Postgraduate training in Hippotherapy and Pilates. Previously worked with Scottish sports teams.

#### Tabitha — Clinic Manager
Handles appointments, client relations, and clinic operations.

## SERVICES

### Physiotherapy
Initial physiotherapy assessments and ongoing treatment plans. Hands-on treatment targeting the root cause of pain and dysfunction.

### Conditions Treated
- Back pain (recurring or chronic)
- Knee pain affecting mobility
- Hip pain and stiffness
- Neck and shoulder pain and tension
- Sports injuries
- Post-surgical joint recovery (hip, knee, shoulder replacements)
- Chronic pain unresponsive to other treatments

### Joint Replacement Preparation and Recovery
Pre- and post-operative physiotherapy for hip, knee, and shoulder replacements.

### Sports Injury Treatment
Assessment and rehabilitation of sports injuries.

### Pilates
Pilates classes available at the clinic.

### Alba MenoStrong Programme
A programme designed for people going through menopause, focusing on strength and wellbeing.
