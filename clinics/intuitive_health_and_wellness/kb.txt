# Intuitive Health & Wellness

## ABOUT THE CLINIC

Intuitive Health & Wellness is located in Huntingdale, Australia. The clinic offers integrative chiropractic care combined with a range of traditional and alternative therapies, designed to restore alignment, support the nervous system, and help the body function at its best.

The clinic was shaped by Dr Zena's personal experience following a childhood injury, where she found healing through chiropractic care. This sparked a desire to help others and led to the creation of the wellness centre.

The philosophy centres on a whole-body approach that integrates the physical, emotional, and energetic systems. The team works collaboratively to facilitate communication between the brain, body, and soul, and to enhance treatment outcomes.

## SERVICES

### Chiropractic Care

The chiropractors at Intuitive Health & Wellness take time to listen and treat the cause of the problem, not only the symptoms. They work with people from all walks of life, supporting children, adolescents, adults, and seniors. The aim is to align the physical, emotional, and chemical aspects of the body.

### Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a holistic healing practice rooted in traditional Chinese medicine. It uses the strategic insertion of fine needles at specific points on the body to restore balance, alleviate pain, and promote overall health and wellness.

### Remedial Massage

Remedial massage is a therapeutic technique that targets specific areas of tension and dysfunction within the body, promoting healing, relaxation, and improved mobility through skilled manipulation of soft tissues and muscles.

### Reiki, Matrix Reimprinting and EFT

Reiki is a holistic healing practice rooted in Japanese tradition that uses gentle touch and energy channelling to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. Practitioners help to balance and restore harmony by tapping into the body's natural energy flow, facilitating relaxation and reducing stress.

### Naturopathy

Naturopaths assess the whole person — mind, body, and spirit — tailoring treatments to individual needs. By addressing the root causes of illness rather than just symptoms, naturopathy promotes overall wellbeing and vitality through herbal medicine, nutrition, and stress management.

### Breathwork and Energetic Healing

Breathwork harnesses the rhythm of the breath to enhance physical, mental, and emotional wellness. Through conscious breathing techniques, individuals can release stress, promote relaxation, and access deeper states of awareness. Breathwork supports personal growth, improves emotional resilience, and facilitates healing by clearing stagnant energy and emotions.

### Kinesiology

Kinesiology is listed among the clinic's core offerings alongside chiropractic, naturopathy, reiki, energy healing, and breathwork.
