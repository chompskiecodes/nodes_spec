## Services

### Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

Northern Physio treats chronic pain, injuries, and musculoskeletal issues. Sessions involve a comprehensive assessment to alleviate pain, improve mobility, and enhance musculoskeletal health. Physiotherapists create personalised plans to support a more active and pain-free lifestyle.

### TAC, Workcover, and DVA

Northern Physio accepts TAC, Workcover, and DVA cases. Services focus on facilitating recovery, restoring function, and supporting rehabilitation. Each session is tailored to help patients navigate the complexities of these schemes and return to optimal health.

### Pre-Employment Functional Assessments

Experienced physiotherapists and exercise physiologists assess an individual's physical capacity to ensure they can safely meet the inherent requirements of a specific job role. The clinic specialises in injury prevention in workplace and personal settings through understanding and promoting functional movement.

## Women's Health

Northern Physio provides specialised physiotherapy for women across all life stages, addressing pelvic health, pregnancy, post-natal recovery, and menopause.

### Pregnancy

Physiotherapy services support women through the physical changes of pregnancy, promoting comfort, strength, and optimal health for both mother and baby.

### Post-Natal Recovery

Post-natal physiotherapy facilitates recovery after childbirth, including regaining strength and managing postpartum discomfort. Services are personalised to support new mothers.

### C-Section Recovery

Specialised physiotherapy for C-section recovery includes targeted exercises, gentle stretches, and guidance to promote healing and reduce discomfort.

### Incontinence

Physiotherapy interventions address urinary incontinence through pelvic floor strengthening exercises, bladder control strategies, and lifestyle modifications. Consistent pelvic floor exercise can substantially decrease leakage and may reduce the need for surgical intervention in stress incontinence cases.

### Prolapse Management

Targeted exercises and personalised strategies are used to alleviate discomfort, improve pelvic floor strength, and improve quality of life for those dealing with prolapse.

### Bowel Disorders

Physiotherapy for bowel disorders includes personalised strategies, exercises, and lifestyle recommendations to manage symptoms and improve bowel function.

### Menopause Support

Specialised services address common menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes, mood swings, and sleep disturbances, as well as pelvic health changes including urinary incontinence and pelvic pain.

## Men's Pelvic Health

Northern Physio offers pelvic floor physiotherapy services specifically for men.

### Prostate Surgery Support

Physiotherapy for men who have undergone prostate surgery addresses post-surgical challenges and promotes recovery. Services include bladder training exercises, pelvic floor relaxation techniques, tracking bladder use, and scheduling toilet breaks to improve bladder control.

### Chronic Pelvic Pain Management

Sessions identify and address root causes of pelvic pain through targeted exercises and manual therapy techniques. The approach includes improving posture, flexibility, and strength in the pelvic region.

### Urinary Incontinence Management

Comprehensive assessment and personalised interventions address urinary incontinence in men, with strategies to regain bladder control and enhance overall pelvic health.

### Bowel Disorders Rehabilitation

Physiotherapists work with men on exercises and lifestyle recommendations to manage bowel disorders and improve pelvic health. Care is provided in a discreet, personalised, and compassionate manner.
