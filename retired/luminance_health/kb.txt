# Luminance Health

## ABOUT LUMINANCE HEALTH

### Clinic Overview

Luminance Health is a holistic chiropractic clinic located in Balgowlah on Sydney's Northern Beaches. The clinic provides gentle, nervous system-led chiropractic care and applied kinesiology for individuals, families, pregnant women, and children of all ages including newborns. Luminance Health is registered with AHPRA.

### About Dr Hannah Leung

Dr Hannah Leung holds a Bachelor of Human Sciences and a Masters in Chiropractic. She has completed further study in paediatrics, pregnancy, Sacro-Occipital Technique (SOT), and Applied Kinesiology (AK). Hannah's approach is grounded in the belief that healing begins with a strong and supported foundation. She views the body as an interconnected system — physical, emotional, and biochemical — and addresses all three to support lasting change.

At the initial consultation, Hannah takes the time to understand your health story, goals, and nervous system. ### Chiropractic Techniques

Hannah uses gentle chiropractic techniques to align the spine and support nervous system function. Her approach is tailored, precise, and supportive — without forceful manoeuvres.

Also known as a chiropractic adjustment, this technique applies precise force to restore postural alignment and relieve pressure on the spine and joints. It supports better movement and reduces pain.

A specialised chiropractic technique focused on the relationship between the sacrum and occiput to restore spinal and nervous system balance.

A whole-body assessment technique using muscle testing to identify imbalances across structural, biochemical, and emotional dimensions of health. See the Holistic Healing section for full detail.

#### Soft Tissue Therapy

Manual therapy to relieve muscular tension and discomfort.

## BABIES AND CHILDREN

### Why Early Care Matters

The first years of life are crucial for nervous system development. Early chiropractic care supports proper spinal alignment, which is essential for healthy nervous system function, immune development, and optimal growth. Birth can be a physically intense experience for babies. Gentle chiropractic care helps address tension or misalignments that may occur during the birth process. Supporting your child's nervous system early helps create the foundation for better sleep, feeding, digestion, and overall development.

### Gentle Adjustments for Babies

Baby chiropractic care uses incredibly gentle pressure — no cracking, no force. Hannah's specialised techniques support nervous system development, helping with colic, feeding difficulties, and sleep issues. The pressure used is similar to gently checking fruit for ripeness. Hannah has specialised training in newborn and infant care, and chiropractic care is safe from birth.

### Signs Your Baby May Benefit

Reflux or digestive discomfort, poor latch or feeding difficulties, unsettled sleep patterns, plagiocephaly (flat head syndrome), preference for turning head one direction, developmental delays or concerns, tongue tie or oral restrictions, and colic.

### Common Conditions in Babies and Children

#### Colic and Reflux

Colic is characterised by prolonged periods of intense, unexplained fussiness and crying in a healthy baby. Reflux involves the backflow of stomach contents into the oesophagus, causing discomfort and irritability. Gentle chiropractic care may help by supporting nervous system control of the digestive system.

#### Difficulty Sleeping

Sleep disturbances can include trouble falling asleep, frequent night awakenings, or waking too early. Chiropractic care may assess whether spinal misalignments are affecting the nervous system and disrupting sleep.

#### Digestive Issues

Digestive issues include constipation, diarrhoea, and irregular bowel movements. Chiropractic adjustments may address potential spinal misalignments that influence digestive function through nerve pathways.

#### Ear Infections

Ear infections (otitis media) are common in children. Some evidence suggests gentle adjustments may support drainage of the ear and reduce frequency of infections.

#### Tongue Ties

When the lingual frenulum is shorter than usual, it can restrict tongue movement and affect feeding, swallowing, and speech. Chiropractic evaluation can help with associated musculoskeletal compensations.

#### Teeth Grinding and Clenching (Bruxism)

May lead to tooth wear, jaw pain, and headaches. Chiropractic care may help relieve muscle tension and promote relaxation.

#### Mouth Breathing

Often caused by nasal congestion, enlarged tonsils, or adenoids. Chiropractic focuses on promoting proper alignment in surrounding structures to potentially improve breathing capacity.

### Neonatal Reflexes

Neonatal reflexes are automatic responses that help babies survive and develop. Most should integrate naturally as the nervous system matures. When reflexes persist beyond their normal timeframe, gentle chiropractic care can support proper integration. Neuro-Emotional Technique (NET) may also be used to support reflex integration and nervous system regulation.

## HOLISTIC HEALING — APPLIED KINESIOLOGY

### What is Applied Kinesiology

Applied Kinesiology (AK) is a technique that uses muscle testing to assess how the body is functioning. It identifies imbalances across three key areas: physical (joint or muscle tension), emotional (stress or stored emotions), and biochemical (nutrition, hormones, and digestion). By observing how muscles respond, valuable insights are gained into how well the brain is communicating with the rest of the body and what it needs to restore balance.

### Structural — Foundation of Health

The structural component focuses on proper alignment of the spine, pelvis, and musculoskeletal system. Through gentle chiropractic adjustments and manual techniques, the physical foundation that supports all other body functions is addressed. When structure is aligned, nerve flow improves, pain decreases, and the body functions more efficiently.

### Biochemical — Nutrition and Healing

The biochemical component addresses nutrition, supplementation, and the chemical processes that fuel the body. Through muscle testing, nutritional deficiencies, food sensitivities, and biochemical imbalances can be identified. Personalised guidance is provided to support the body's biochemical needs naturally.

### Emotional — Mind-Body Connection

The emotional component recognises that stress, trauma, and emotional imbalances can manifest as physical symptoms. Through gentle techniques, stored emotional patterns that may be affecting health are identified and released. When emotional stress is addressed, the nervous system can return to a state of regulation, allowing for deeper healing.

### Neuro-Emotional Technique (NET)

Where appropriate, Neuro-Emotional Technique (NET) is incorporated to help the nervous system release stored stress patterns and support mind-body balance. NET is a gentle, mind-body approach to releasing unresolved stress patterns.

### Conditions Supported by Holistic Healing

Sympathetic dominance (fight or flight), digestive stress and gut health issues, immune system dysfunction, emotional stress and anxiety, sleep disturbances, postural imbalances, chronic pain patterns, headaches and migraines, and other stress-related conditions.

### Benefits of Holistic Healing

Restores clear communication between the brain and body to support healing. Gently releases stored stress and supports a calmer, more balanced emotional state. Focuses on root causes rather than short-term symptom relief, promoting lasting wellness.

### Applied Kinesiology and Stress

AK can help with stress and fatigue by addressing structural, biochemical, and emotional factors that contribute to these conditions. The whole-body approach helps identify and address root causes rather than just managing symptoms.

## FAQ

#### What conditions can chiropractic care help with

Back and neck pain, headaches, joint pain, poor posture, sports injuries, pregnancy-related discomfort, digestive issues, ear infections in children, sleep disturbances, and nervous system stress. #### Is chiropractic care safe during pregnancy

Yes. Hannah provides specialised pregnancy chiropractic care that is safe and gentle throughout all stages of pregnancy.

#### Do adjustments hurt

Chiropractic care is generally not painful. Most patients experience gentle adjustments that promote healing and wellness without discomfort. Any mild soreness that may occur is typically temporary and resolves quickly.

#### Do adjustments hurt babies or children

No. Adjustments for babies and children use extremely gentle pressure — no cracking, no force. The pressure is similar to gently checking fruit for ripeness.

#### What age can babies start chiropractic care

Babies can start chiropractic care as early as a few days old. Gentle adjustments can support their development and address any discomfort from birth-related stresses.

#### What signs indicate my child might benefit from care

Feeding difficulties, sleep issues, excessive fussiness, developmental delays, head shape concerns (plagiocephaly), or preferential turning of the head. Luminance Health also supports children with focus issues, posture problems, and sports injuries.

#### Is applied kinesiology backed by science

Applied kinesiology is used as a complementary assessment tool. By observing muscle responses, imbalances across structural, biochemical, and emotional dimensions are identified to guide personalised treatment.
