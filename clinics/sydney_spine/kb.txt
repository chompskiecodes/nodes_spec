## PAIN CONDITIONS OVERVIEW

### Conditions Treated

#### Shoulder Pain
Often we try to ignore niggling shoulder injuries: sometimes it only hurts when you move a certain way or complete a certain task. The best way is to determine the origin of your discomfort and treat it — we can help you do this in a manner suitable to your lifestyle.

#### Hip Pain
The hip joint is generally robust. After repeated wear and tear it can become irritated just like any other part of our body. If your hips are sore, we can investigate the cause of the issue and help.

#### Poor Posture
Prolonged poor posture (especially in sitting) may encourage poor breathing, poor circulation, physical soreness, low motivation and fatigue. We can optimise your posture and sitting position so these symptoms are in the past.

#### Sciatica
If you're feeling shooting pain down your leg, you might be experiencing Sciatica. We can help alleviate pain in the long term.

#### Knee Pain
If you can't take a step forward without wincing, we can help you get back on track with pain-relieving and effective chiropractic treatment that suits your lifestyle.

### FAQ

#### Is Chiro covered by Medicare Australia?
Yes. If your GP has filled out an Enhanced Primary Care (EPC) referral form, Medicare can rebate a portion of up to 5 Chiropractic Treatments per calendar year. You can't claim your Private Health Insurance Extras cover on the same day.

## BACK PAIN

### About Back Pain
Back pain is very common. It can be caused by muscle or ligament injury, bulging or ruptured discs, or arthritis. The prevalence of back pain across all ages can be linked to society's slide into sedentary lifestyle habits — poor posture, unconscious slouching, and incorrect muscular control all contribute to the wear and tear of your body.

Our chiropractic team has over 30 years' experience in leading back pain treatment in Sydney. We solve acute or chronic back pain by investigating the root of the problem and suggesting proper treatment options. After diagnosis, our back pain solutions include a bespoke treatment and management plan to suit your Sydney lifestyle.

### Our Team
In Double Bay, Dr Gabi Fransman is our chiropractor and Pilates instructor focused on back pain solutions that help prevent injuries across all ages and abilities. Her tip: "There is nothing more important than movement for your mind and body. Make being active fun — not a chore. It doesn't matter what exercise you choose. Just keep moving!"

### When to Seek Treatment
Consider having your pain assessed by a chiropractor or health care professional if:
- Pain persists after initial home treatment
- It's severe, or doesn't improve after rest
- It spreads down one or both knees (like sciatica)

### FAQ

#### Should I go to my GP for back pain?
In Australia you can consult your GP for back pain, but manual therapists (Chiropractors, Physiotherapists and Osteopaths) have the ability to take a history, examine you, formulate a diagnosis, and prescribe appropriate treatment, or refer if necessary.

#### Who should I call if my back hurts?
Get a recommendation from friends, your GP, or family to go to someone with a reputation for managing your back condition. For back issues, Chiropractors, Physiotherapists and Osteopaths are generally the best practitioners to visit.

#### How much does it cost to treat back pain?
It depends on the complexity and duration of your injury. Most uncomplicated low back pain (no referral down the thigh) can be resolved in 6–12 visits at a good manual medicine clinic.

#### Can I go to Emergency for back pain?
Yes, if you have excruciating and intractable pain, Emergency is a plausible option. If you experience numbness in your private parts, or any bladder or bowel incontinence, you should be assessed in hospital immediately.

## HEADACHE TREATMENT

### About Headaches
Headaches can be caused by various factors including stress, poor posture, teeth clenching, muscle tension, and joint dysfunction. Tension headaches, migraines, and cervicogenic headaches are the most common types we treat. By addressing root causes through chiropractic care, we can help reduce the frequency and severity of your pain.

### Treatment Techniques

#### Motorised Flexion Distraction Therapy (Spinal Disc Decompression)
Doctor-controlled traction technique that targets specific levels of the spine. It gently separates the vertebrae, reducing pressure on spinal discs and nerves — alleviating headache symptoms caused by cervical spine issues.

#### Diversified Chiropractic Technique
Addresses biomechanical dysfunctions or joint injuries in each section of the spine. Uses a high-velocity, low-amplitude thrust that is safe, painless, and effective. Corrects spinal joint dysfunction to help reduce headache frequency and intensity.

#### Activator Device Adjusting
Handheld instrument that delivers a precise, gentle thrust to areas of injury or joint dysfunction. Avoids forceful manual manipulation — suitable for patients of all ages, from newborns to seniors. Ideal for those seeking a non-invasive approach to headache relief.

#### Muscle Release and Trigger Point Release
Targeted pressure and stretching to release muscle tension and trigger points that contribute to headache pain. Promotes relaxation and improves blood flow, helping to alleviate tension headaches.

#### Myofascial Dry Needling (Western Acupuncture)
Fine needles inserted into specific muscle points to induce relaxation. Effectively reduces muscle tension and provides significant relief from headaches caused by muscular issues.

#### Postural Retraining and Rehabilitation
Chiropractors provide postural retraining advice to improve workstation ergonomics and everyday posture. Correcting postural imbalances achieves longer-lasting headache relief.

### Benefits of Chiropractic Care for Headaches
- Reduce frequency and severity of headaches
- Improve spinal alignment and function
- Alleviate muscle tension and trigger points
- Enhance overall posture and ergonomics
- Promote long-term pain relief and prevention

### FAQ

#### What types of headaches can chiropractic care help with?
Tension headaches, migraines, and cervicogenic headaches. By addressing underlying causes such as spinal joint dysfunction, muscle tension, and poor posture, our treatments help reduce frequency and severity.

#### Is chiropractic care safe for headache treatment?
Yes. Our evidence-based techniques are non-invasive and designed to provide gentle and effective relief. Treatments are tailored to each patient's specific needs.

#### Are chiropractic treatments painful?
No. Techniques such as Activator Device Adjusting and gentle spinal adjustments are designed to provide pain-free relief. Patients typically feel relief and relaxation after treatments.

#### How soon can I expect results?
This varies depending on the severity and cause of your headaches. Some patients experience relief after a few sessions; others may need ongoing treatment. Our chiropractors will create a personalised treatment plan.

## NECK PAIN

### About Neck Pain
Neck pain and stiffness is the second most common condition we treat. A leading cause is increased time on electronic devices like laptops and tablets, which puts a huge strain on the neck. The younger you are when you start using devices with poor posture, the harder it is to manage and retrain neck muscles. Neck issues can result in headaches, neck stiffness, chest, mid back, or arm pain.

At Sydney Spine and Sports Clinic we go beyond treating symptoms and aim to find the root cause. We tailor a chiropractic treatment plan to suit your lifestyle.

### FAQ

#### Are chiropractors good for neck pain?
Yes. Chiropractors specialise in all aspects of the back and spine, and the neck is no exception. We will find the proper solution for your pain relief.

#### Can a chiropractor fix a stiff neck?
Manual practitioners don't "fix" anything. Our approach is to identify why the neck is stiff, loosen up the joints and muscles, and then discuss strategies to keep the neck moving better — through better posture, strengthening exercises, or activity modification.

#### Is massage or a chiropractor better for neck pain?
The main difference is that chiropractors can make a diagnosis, making us first-line practitioners. The most important aspect of any treatment is a correct diagnosis — ruling out contraindications or underlying pathologies before applying treatment. Patients should seek a proper diagnosis prior to getting a massage.
