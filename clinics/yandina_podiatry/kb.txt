Eligibility

A valid GP Chronic Condition Management Plan (GPCCMP) referral from your GP is required

Medicare provides up to 5 subsidised allied health visits yearly, subject to remaining available visits

Most routine podiatry appointments are fully covered under the standard 20-minute consultation

See our FAQ section or contact us if you are unsure about your eligibility.

---

[FEE & ELIGIBILITY CLARIFICATION — pensioners, concessions, and bulk billing]

Consultation fees are fixed and depend on the reason for the visit, not on pensioner or concession card status:

- Heel pain or foot pain: Initial consultation, approximately 40 minutes, $120.
- Skin, nail, or ingrown toenail concerns (corns, calluses, routine nail care, ingrown toenails): Initial consultation, approximately 20–30 minutes, $80.

There is no pensioner or concession discount on these fees. A patient with a valid GP referral under a Medicare Chronic Disease Management (CDM) Plan can be bulk billed — this depends on having a CDM referral, not on pension or concession card status alone.

Bulk billing is available only with a valid GP referral under a Medicare CDM Plan. For questions about what a CDM Plan is or how to get one, patients should speak with their GP (who can confirm eligibility and arrange the referral) or check our website for more information — this clinic does not confirm CDM eligibility over the phone.
