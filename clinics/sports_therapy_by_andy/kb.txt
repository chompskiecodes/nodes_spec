# Sports Therapy by Andy

## About

Sports Therapy by Andy is a sports and remedial massage therapy practice based in North Strathfield, Sydney, Australia. The practice specialises in treating back pain, sciatica, and musculoskeletal injuries using safe, proven manual therapy techniques — without surgery, cortisone injections, pain medication, or spinal adjustments.

## Services

### Pain Relief

Fast and effective treatment for back, neck, and shoulder pain, including chronic back pain and sciatica. The practice offers a free 15-minute consultation for prospective patients wanting to learn more before booking.

### Injury Recovery

Treatment to help muscles and joints heal safely following sports injuries or accidents.

### Posture and Mobility Improvement

Therapy to correct posture, ease muscle tension, and restore natural movement and range of motion.

---

## Can Sports/Remedial Massage Help with Back Pain?

Yes, sports and remedial massage can help with back pain. Benefits include:

- Helps reduce pain
- Relaxes tight muscles
- Reduces stress
- Improves movement
- Helps you feel better overall
