## ABOUT SPEEDING HEALTH

### Overview
Speeding Health is a family-run brother and sister business based in Adeyfield, Hemel Hempstead, Hertfordshire. Glen Chesterton and Rachel Chesterton have lived in the area the majority of their lives. They specialise in helping the community's mobility and spinal health through their 3M Process.

### The 3M Process

#### Mobilise
The first phase after assessment focuses on mobilising the body to reach its best potential. Primarily using massage and other techniques to loosen up the joints, preparing for the Movement phase.

#### Movement
"Rest is Rust and Motion is Lotion" — Movement recommendations are tailored to specific needs to keep clients moving and doing the things they love and need to do.

#### Maintain
The final phase is maintaining the body at its best. This involves determining the right regular treatment pattern (fortnightly, monthly, or as needed) and providing exercises to self-manage.

## FAQ

#### Is Speeding Health suitable for all ages?
Yes. The clinic helps clients of all ages improve mobility, manage pain, and maintain better health through the 3M process.

#### Who is Rachel's massage available for?
Rachel's sports and remedial massage is available for female clients only.

#### What does the Initial Consultation with Glen include?
The Initial Consultation and Treatment is a 60-minute appointment that includes a full assessment of your condition followed by initial treatment.

#### What is the difference between Clinical Therapy and Sports Massage with Glen?
Clinical Therapy is a structured treatment programme involving assessment, diagnosis, and rehabilitation planning — suitable for clients with injuries or musculoskeletal conditions. Sports Massage is a standalone hands-on session focused on muscle recovery and performance.

#### Do I need a referral to book?
No referral is needed. You can book directly online or by calling the clinic.

#### What is the Report of Findings appointment?
The Report of Findings is a follow-up session after your Initial Consultation with Glen. Glen reviews the assessment findings and agrees a treatment plan with you.

#### What happens in a free discovery visit?
A free 20-minute in-person visit to discuss your health goals and concerns with Glen. No treatment is given — it is a chance to find out if Speeding Health is the right fit for you.

#### Where is Speeding Health located?
The main clinic is at 51 Turners Hill, Adeyfield, Hemel Hempstead, HP2 4LH. Glen also sees clients at Adeyfield Free Church (Maylands Plaza, HP2 4GZ). Rachel also sees clients at Clockhouse Court, 11 London Road, St Albans, AL1 1LA.

#### How often should I come?
After your initial sessions Glen or Rachel will recommend a maintenance schedule tailored to you — this could be fortnightly, monthly, or as needed.

#### Is parking available?
Please contact the clinic directly for parking information at each location.
