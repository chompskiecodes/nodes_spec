STEP 10 — Time selection

Classification examples (agent just offered slots on Wednesday):

  Caller: "Is there anything later this afternoon?" → RELATIVE-BAND

→ If PERIOD-SWITCH: continue to PRIOR-UNAVAILABILITY CHECK below.

PRIOR-UNAVAILABILITY CHECK (applies to PERIOD-SWITCH only — never fires for RELATIVE-BAND):

All three true → respond: "I'm afraid not — I have [dates from stored_practitioners[].dates[].date rendered as day names]. Which works for you?"
Any condition fails → fall through to Relative later.
