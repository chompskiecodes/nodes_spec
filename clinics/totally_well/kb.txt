## ABOUT TOTALLY WELL

### Overview
Totally Well is a holistic health clinic located at the Easy T Centre in Robina, Gold Coast, Queensland. The clinic specialises in Clinical Manual Lymphatic Drainage, Complex Lymphatic Therapy, Lymphoedema and Lipoedema Management, Post-Surgical Care, Remedial Massage and Rehabilitation, Myofascial and Cellulite Therapy, and Naturopathy and Natural Medicine.

All therapists have advanced lymphatic training and are aligned with Australasian Lymphology Association (ALA) standards. The clinic's evidence-informed, whole-body approach supports fluid retention, post-surgical recovery, lymphoedema, lipoedema, chronic fatigue, gut health, hormonal imbalance, immune concerns, and nervous system regulation.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### What is Clinical Manual Lymphatic Drainage?
Clinical Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) is a specialised, gentle therapy designed to stimulate lymph flow, reduce swelling, and support detoxification and immune function. At Totally Well, MLD is performed by clinically trained therapists.

### Do you treat lymphoedema and lipoedema?
Yes. Totally Well provides lymphatic care for lymphoedema and lipoedema, including early-stage support and ongoing management. Treatments are tailored to each patient's condition, symptoms, and medical history, and may form part of a broader decongestive approach.

### Is lymphatic drainage safe after surgery?
Yes, when performed correctly. Clinical MLD can be safely introduced as early as a few days post-surgery, depending on the procedure and clearance. Early intervention helps reduce swelling, discomfort, and fibrosis — supporting faster, smoother recovery.

### Are your therapists clinically trained?
Yes. All therapists are qualified Remedial Massage Therapists with formal training in Decongestive Lymphatic Therapy, including post-surgical care, scar tissue management, and fascia-lymph interactions. Lymphatic training is endorsed by the Australasian Lymphology Association (ALA), and all treatments are delivered within evidence-informed clinical standards.

### Do you accept private health insurance?
Yes. Totally Well offers HICAPS on the spot for eligible services under remedial massage and clinical lymphatic therapies, depending on the patient's fund and level of cover. Note: Services with Bartosz (Bart) Kulikowski are not HICAPS claimable.

### What conditions can lymphatic drainage help with?
Lymphatic drainage and the clinic's whole-body approach supports: fluid retention, post-surgical recovery, lymphoedema, lipoedema, chronic fatigue, gut health, hormonal imbalance, immune concerns, and nervous system regulation. The clinic also supports recovery from cosmetic and reconstructive surgery.
