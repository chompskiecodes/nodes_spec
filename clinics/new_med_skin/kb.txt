# MedSkin

## ABOUT

MedSkin is a medical-led aesthetic practice located in Shellharbour, NSW, Australia. Led by a Brazilian-trained medical practitioner with over 20 years of clinical experience, the clinic offers a dedicated approach to aesthetic medicine combining long-standing medical expertise with personalised care and thorough clinical assessments.

Dr Rachel D. Leoni is an experienced medical practitioner with over 20 years of clinical experience across diverse medical fields. Originally trained in Brazil, she has a substantial background in general medicine, emergency care, and skin health.

## SERVICES

### New Patient Consultation

Comprehensive initial consultation to clarify concerns and create a personalised aesthetic plan and treatment.

### PRF Facial Packages

Facial packages including sessions of Platelet-Rich Fibrin (PRF), Microneedling, and LED light therapy.

### PRF Hair Packages

Hair packages including sessions of Platelet-Rich Fibrin (PRF), Microneedling, and LED light therapy.

### Peelings

Various peeling techniques to keep the skin healthy, from acne and oil control to anti-ageing peels.

### LED Light Therapy

Non-invasive and pain-free LED light treatments suitable for most skin types.

### Wrinkles and Lines

Professional assessments for those seeking to soften facial lines or manage clinical conditions related to muscle activity.

### Volume and Contouring

Consultations for facial contouring and volume enhancement to address areas of concern.

### Liftera (HIFU)

Non-surgical facial and neck lifting using High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound technology.

### NCTF BOOST 135HA

Skin booster and anti-ageing poly-revitalising complex treatment.
