# Village Remedies

## ABOUT

Village Remedies is a natural health clinic offering Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine, Massage Therapy, and Physiotherapy across three Sydney locations.

The clinic practices distal needling in a Dr. Tan and Master Tung style, using Jimmy Cheng style pulse diagnosis. All practitioners share the same training and style to ensure consistent quality of care across locations.

## LOCATIONS

### Balmain

Located in Balmain, Sydney.

### Lane Cove

Located on Elizabeth Street, Lane Cove, Sydney.

### Marrickville

Located on Gordon Street, Marrickville, Sydney.

## SERVICES

### Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine

Village Remedies specialises in distal acupuncture using Dr. Tan and Master Tung styles, combined with Jimmy Cheng pulse diagnosis. Initial (new patient) and follow-up (returning patient) appointments are available.

### Massage Therapy

Remedial massage therapy available at all locations.

### Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy available at all locations.
