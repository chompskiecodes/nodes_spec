# Meraki Holistic Health

## ABOUT

Meraki Holistic Health is a holistic health clinic based in Australia. The clinic takes a whole-person approach to healthcare, uniting Chinese medicine, herbal medicine, acupuncture, and bodywork to support physical, mental, and spiritual healing. Treatments are tailored to each individual's unique needs, promoting optimal health and well-being. The environment is designed to be safe and calming, supported by a compassionate team and a strong community focus.

The clinic's philosophy centres on empowering clients rather than chasing quick fixes. Practitioners hold space for long-term, intentional healing — whether a client is struggling to conceive, seeking an energetic realignment, or simply wanting a restorative massage.
