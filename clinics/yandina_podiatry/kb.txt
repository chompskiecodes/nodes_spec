# Yandina Podiatry

Yandina Podiatry is a podiatry clinic located inside Farrell Street Family Practice, Shop 6, Yandina Shopping Centre, 1 Old Gympie Road, Yandina QLD 4561. Bulk billed appointments are available with a valid GP Chronic Disease Management (CDM) referral.

Email: admin@yandinapodiatry.com.au

## SERVICES

### General Foot Care
Routine podiatry care helping maintain healthy, comfortable feet and prevent ongoing foot problems. Includes: toenail trimming, thickened nails, corns and calluses, dry cracked heels, general skin care, and ongoing preventative foot maintenance.

### Foot Pain & Injury Treatment
Assessment and treatment for a wide range of foot and lower limb conditions including: heel pain, plantar fasciitis, arch pain, forefoot pain, Achilles pain, tendon and muscle strain, walking discomfort, and overuse injuries.

### Orthotics & Foot Support
Assessment and fitting for: custom orthotics, pre-made orthotics, arch support devices, pressure relief solutions, and footwear support recommendations.

### Sports & Active Foot Care
Podiatry care for active patients and sports-related concerns including: sports-related foot pain, running injuries, heel and arch pain, foot fatigue, footwear advice, pressure management, and injury prevention.

### Diabetic Foot Care
Preventative diabetic foot care including: diabetic foot assessments, circulation testing, sensation testing, skin and nail care, pressure area management, footwear guidance, and ongoing preventative monitoring.

### Skin & Toenail Care
Treatment for: ingrown toenails, thickened toenails, fungal nails, cracked heels, dry skin, and general nail and skin concerns.

## HOME VISITS

Yandina Podiatry offers home visit podiatry services for patients who have difficulty attending the clinic due to mobility, health, or transport limitations. Home visits are available for selected areas across the Sunshine Coast Hinterland. Services include professional routine foot care and ongoing podiatry support.

## AGED CARE & RETIREMENT VILLAGES

Yandina Podiatry provides podiatry services for retirement village residents, independent living communities, and aged care facilities across Yandina and the Sunshine Coast Hinterland. Services include routine foot care, diabetic foot assessments, nail care, skin care, mobility concerns, and ongoing podiatry management.

## CLINIC ACCESS

Convenient nearby parking. Easy flat-surface access. Minimal walking distance to clinic entrance. Suitable for elderly and patients with mobility needs.

## FEES & BILLING

Consultation fees depend on the reason for the visit:
- Heel pain or foot pain: initial consultation approximately 40 minutes — $120
- Skin, nail, or ingrown toenail concerns (corns, calluses, routine nail care, ingrown toenails): initial consultation approximately 20–30 minutes — $80

There is no pensioner or concession discount on standard fees.

Bulk billing is available only with a valid GP referral under a Medicare Chronic Disease Management (CDM) Plan. Medicare provides up to 5 subsidised allied health visits per year under this plan. Most routine podiatry appointments are fully covered under the standard 20-minute consultation. Patients should speak with their GP to confirm eligibility and arrange the referral.
