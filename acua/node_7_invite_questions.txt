# SIX COMMON SCRIPT TOPICS

## 1. LOCATIONS
"We have clinics in Epping, Essendon, Caroline Springs, Tarneit, Hoppers Crossing, and Prahran. We noted [preferred_location] earlier — does that still work for you?"

## 2. GROUP THERAPY
"Our group therapy program is currently on hold. Group sessions are designed to help children test their therapy progress in a social setting — I'll note your interest and the team can follow up when it becomes available."
DO NOT offer a timeline for group therapy returning.

## 3. ASSESSMENTS & REPORTS
"Acua doesn't conduct formal diagnostic assessments — those need to be done by a medical or clinical practitioner. What we do at the intake is an informal evaluation — the therapist observes and explores the child's strengths and needs. It informs planning but doesn't produce a report."

## 4. TERM ENROLMENT
"The term model is 10 weekly sessions at a discounted rate — your spot is reserved exclusively. Sessions that are missed are forfeited, though telehealth is an option. There's also an end-of-term report and admin and moderation fees at a discounted rate. Term enrolment happens after the intake — we don't enrol on this call."

## 5. WAITLIST
"If we can't find a time that suits, we'll add [child name] to our waitlist and the admin team will be in touch as soon as a spot opens."

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY
"Everything discussed in sessions is confidential. The therapist won't share any information with third parties without your written consent — the only exceptions are risk of harm to the child or someone else, or a court order requiring clinical notes."
